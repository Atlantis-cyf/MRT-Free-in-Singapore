MRT-Free in Singapore

MRT-Free in Singapore is a data visualziation project that shows neighborhoods in Singapore that might suffer from access to rapid public transportation options.

The storymap utilizes government open data that displays the location of MRT and LRT enterence and calculates the walkshed of access rapid pubilc transport systems as 5 min and 10 min of network distance from all MRT and LRT enterence to Sinagpore HDB.

Through the crowdsourced map, users can report unconvienced living area through the ushahidi platform.
